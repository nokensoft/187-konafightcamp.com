WHO WE ARE

ABOUT US

Kona Fight Camp is Bali's premier Muay Thai training camp, welcoming fighters and fitness enthusiasts from around the world. Whether you're a seasoned competitor or stepping into the gym for the first time, our professional coaches will push you to your limits.

Our camp offers world-class training in a supportive community environment. We cover everything from fundamentals — technique, bag work, pad work — to advanced sparring, clinching, and competition preparation.
